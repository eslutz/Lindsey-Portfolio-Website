Lettuce Help Restaurant Website

Coursera UX project
Background

Design a restaurant website with ordering for the Charlotte, NC area. The target audience is young parents.

Elevator pitch - Bringing healthy flavorful foods to busy families in the Charlotte area. Giving parents a break and nothing to feel guilty about. Lettuce help you.


Target Audience

Parents of young children and babysitters.


Demographics
  • Gender - more women than men.
  • Education - college educated.
  • Occupations - office workers, tech employees.
  • Age - late 20's to 40's.
  • Personality - health consience.
  • Attitude - loves the outdoors.
  • Values - family focused, moderatley liberal, middle class.
  • Lifestyle - family, weekend adventures.

User Needs
  • Find out if the restaurant delivers to their area.
  • Order food online and customize food.
  • Save favorite foods to quickly order again.
  • Schedule delivery in advance.
  • Follow the order.
  • Share with friends.
  • Contact if they need help of have questions.

Client Needs
  • To sell food online that will be delivered.
  • Provide a system for the order customization.
  • Provide an easy to use system.
  • Include fun activities for kids.
  • Include information on the food.

Movist - Moving App

See the prototype.
Background

Movist is a fictional app that makes moving easier than ever. Find movers, boxes, moving tips, and even things to new in your new area with just a swipe of your finger. Get organized with moving lists and a fun countdown.


Functions
  • Find movers, boxes, etc.
  • Lists for things to do before moving, things to donate and sell, etc.
  • Find things to do in your new area.
  • Moving tips.

Potential Users
  • Students going off to college.
  • Employee transferring to a new location.
  • Family moving across the country.
  • New retiree moving to be closer to family.
